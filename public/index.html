About Me

I am Charles and I am many things beside a web developer (though I often find myself humming Kip's ode to technology from the movie Napolean Dynamite). I am a martial artist, veteran, budding author, amatuer comedian, gamer, bassist, family man, public speaker. I am and do many things (perhaps too many) but at the root of it all I am a man that yearns for growth. I am a lifelong learner that spends more time researching, contemplating, and practicing than I spend watching tv and I do my best to motivate others to chase after their own dreams. All too often people listen to that inner critic that shouts negativity into their ear and I do whatever I can to help silence those voices. The truth is that we can all reach our goals, as long as we are willing to put in the work to make it happen.
